## Overview

v1.1 is the validation-hardening milestone. Every product was driven to an unambiguous per-region PASS, honest FAIL with named upgrade path, or deferral with a dated unblock condition — using OPERA / EGMS / JRC / EMS as validators, not quality ceilings.

Cross-cutting infrastructure was rebuilt from scratch: reproducible conda-forge environment, shared validation harness, macOS multiprocessing bundle, subprocess watchdog, immutable criteria module, and manifest-driven results matrix. Seven product validation cells now report product-quality and reference-agreement as structurally separate columns.
